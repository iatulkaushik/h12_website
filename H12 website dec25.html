About Hostel 12

Est. 2003 · Powai, Mumbai

Hostel 12 is a vibrant residential community at IIT Bombay.

"Crown of the Campus," standing true to its name, is the largest post-graduate hostel at IIT Bombay, with its four wings spread across 10,000+ square feet of area. Nestled between the Powai and the Vihar lakes, the lush green premise of the hostel provides a serene and scenic environment for the residents. Equipped with modern amenities and blessed with natural beauty, the hostel continues to be a partner in the prosperity of all its inmates. This website forms an interactive platform for the hostellers to share updates, capture memories, and register issues with the council of Hostel 12.

You can also write to us at gsech12@iitb.ac.in
